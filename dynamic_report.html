Analysing exposure to Instagram clothing ads and how the may influence consumer behaviour

This report analyses participants Instagram average daily usage, how often they are exposed to Instagram advertisements regarding clothing websites, how different aspects of these advertisements influence participants to click on websites, and their purchasing behaviour. We were interested in seeing how much Instagram advertisements impact people’s purchases because we wanted to be able to analyse what specific aspects of these ads influenced people to click, and also buy. The data collected may be useful to advertisement companies, clothing stores and anyone interested in consumer behaviour.

Summary value number 1

The average daily Instagram screen time is 1.4 hours.

Summary value number 2

Out of 20 participants, 12 clicked on an advertisement.

Summary value number 3

6 participants both clicked on an advertisement and made a purchase.
